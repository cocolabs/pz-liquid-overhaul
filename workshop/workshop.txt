description=
description=A mod presented by the Coco Labs community focusing on increasing the possibilities with the game liquids. Store liquid in a "Water Jug" and "Large Gas Can". Take and Place Water Jugs on any dispenser to refill them.

description=[h1]Feature:[/h1]
description=- Renamed Big Water Bottle to Water Jug.
description=- Complete re-design of the Water Jug and Dispenser.
description=- 3D Water Jug for animations and equipped item.
description=- Dispensers removable/replaceable bottles.
description=- Water/Fue Dispensers become Empty Dispensers when depleted.
description=- Pourable Gas Can and fillable with Water.
description=- Water Jug can now collect rain water.
description=
description=
description=[hr]
description=
description=[h1]Context Menu Addition[/h1]
description=- More vanilla looking context menu options.
description=- All water and fuel container show the amount of unit of liquid contained.
description=- Pourable Gas Can and can now contain water too.
description=
description=
description=[hr]
description=
description=[h1]Large Gas Can[/h1]
description=- Weight 15 when full.
description=- Capacity of 20 unit of liquid. (Twice as the vanilla gas can)
description=- Can be filled with Water, Tainted Water & Fuel.
description=- Can Add/Siphon Gasoline on vehicles.
description=- Can be found at the same locations as the vanilla gas can.
description=
description=
description=[hr]
description=
description=[h1]Water Jug[/h1]
description=- Visible 3D model.
description=- Weight 30 when full.
description=- Capacity of 100 unit of liquid. (Changed from 250 to 100 for balance)
description=- Can be filled with Water, Tainted Water & Fuel.
description=- Can be found in most groceries in fridges and on water dispensers.
description=- Can collect rain water.
description=
